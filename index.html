Facial Sculpting & Buccal Massage

Buccal massage is a specialized intraoral technique where gentle pressure is applied from inside the mouth to sculpt and tone the facial muscles. It's incredibly effective for defining cheekbones, lifting the jawline, and reducing puffiness — all naturally, without any invasive procedures.

For optimal results, we recommend starting with weekly sessions for the first 4-6 weeks, then transitioning to maintenance treatments every 2-4 weeks. Roza will create a personalized schedule based on your specific goals and skin response.

Most clients find the TMJ Sculpt deeply relaxing. You may feel some pressure in areas of tension, but Roza adjusts the intensity based on your comfort level. Many clients report immediate relief from jaw tension and headaches.

Body Contouring

Ultrasonic cavitation uses low-frequency sound waves to break down fat cells, while radiofrequency (RF) lifting uses heat energy to tighten skin and stimulate collagen production. Combined with vacuum therapy, this treatment provides comprehensive body contouring with visible results.

Most facial treatments have zero downtime — you'll look refreshed and glowing immediately! For body contouring treatments, you may experience mild redness or warmth that subsides within a few hours. You can return to normal activities right away.

Most clients see visible results after their first session. For optimal results, we recommend 6-10 sessions spaced 1-2 weeks apart. Roza will create a personalized plan during your consultation based on your goals.

Pressotherapy

Pressotherapy is a lymphatic drainage treatment that uses air-pressure technology applied through specialized garments. It promotes detoxification, reduces water retention and bloating, improves circulation, and helps with cellulite. It's deeply relaxing and leaves you feeling lighter immediately.
